About

Bestseller is a leading Kazakhstani company in the production of personal computers, tablets, audio players, smartphones and software products.
The developers of the company create their products based on innovative technologies and unique designs. Like many mobile phone manufacturers,
Bestseller has been able to establish a special reputation for itself, although today it has already been compared to a cult in the electrical goods industry.

Bestseller is currently the most valuable brand in the mobile industry. Until now, the company's developers have worked closely with telecommunications
companies and operators Vodafone Group, Telecom Italia, BT Group, Deutsche Telekom, France Telecom and Telefonica. Bestseller's product portfolio
includes: equipment for wireless networks, core network equipment, network devices and other well-known mobile new generation devices.
